Environmental Benefits of Recycling:

Material Energy Savings Environmental Impact Natural Resource Savings Other
Recycling one aluminum saves enough energy to run a TV for three hours Reduces 95% of pollution One pound of recycled aluminum saves four pounds of bauxite We can rebuild our commercial air fleet four times every year by using all the aluminum that is thrown away
Recycling one glass container saves enough energy to light a 100-watt bulb for four hours 20% less air pollution; 50% less water pollution one ton of glass made from 50% recycled materials saves 250 pounds of mining waste Glass can be reused an infinite number of times; over 41 billion glass containers are made per year
60% energy savings Each recycled ton saves 60 pounds of air pollution Each recycled ton saves 17 trees and 7000 gallons of water Every year enough paper is thrown away to make a 12 foot wall from New York to California
Plastic milk containers are only hald the weight they were in 1960 Saves waste, which emits toxic pollutants or irritants into the air If every plastic bottle used was recycled, two billion tons of plastic would be kept out of landfills We use enough plastic every year to cover Texas
One pound of steel recycled saves enough energy to light a 60-watt bulb for a day Every year we create 11.5 million tons of ferrous waste One ton of recycled steel saves 2,500 pounds of ore, 100 pounds of coal, and 40 pounds of limestone Enough iron and steel is thrown away to continually supply the nation's automakers
